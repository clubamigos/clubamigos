About Club Amigos

Club Amigos was first registered under the NGO Act on 7th February 2011. After the introduction of the Commissioner of Sports, it was re-registered under that act on 30th October 2017.

Since its establishment, the club has actively participated in 3rd Division Tournaments, FA Cups which was organized by FAM, as well as Futsal and Street Soccer competitions.

Our most notable achievements include reaching the semi-finals of the Independence Day Futsal Tournament organized by the Ministry of Youth & Sports, as well as the highly competitive “Abo” Futsal Tournament — an event that featured many of the top players in the country.
